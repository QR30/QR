Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and would like to inform you that our website does not collect any personal information from visitors.

1. No Data Collection:

This website does not request any personally identifiable information such as name or email address. There are no registration forms, login forms, or comment sections.

2. External Links:

The site contains links to external platforms such as YouTube, Instagram, and others. When you click on these links, the privacy policies of those websites apply to you.

3. Cookies and Google Analytics:

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics collects non-personal data such as browser type, session duration, and pages visited using cookies. This data is not shared with any third party.

4. Updates:

This policy may be updated if new features are added to the website in the future. Any significant changes will be clearly stated here.
