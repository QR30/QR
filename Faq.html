Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about Obadah Mohammad Adnan's channel.

Are the recitations your own voice

Yes, all recitations are in my own voice, Alhamdulillah.

How can I contact the channel's management

You can contact me via the "Contact Us" page, email, or Instagram.

Can I share the videos with others

Yes, you may share the videos as long as you don't modify them.

What Qira'ah (recitation style) do you use

I mostly recite in Hafs 'an 'Asim, and may post other Qira'at in the future, In shaa Allah.

Do you accept requests for specific verses or surahs

Yes, I'm happy to receive your requests via comments or Instagram.

How often do you upload new videos

I aim to upload daily. It's recommended to turn on the notification bell to stay updated.

Do you do live streams

There may be live streams in the near future, In shaa Allah, for recitations or reflections.

Is the content suitable for memorization and review

Yes, the recitations are clear and calm, ideal for memorizing and reviewing.

How can I support you

1- Subscribe to the channel, turn on notifications, and engage with the content.
2- Click the "Support Me" button to visit my Patreon page.

Can I use your recitations in my videos or projects

Yes, under two conditions:
1. No editing or distortion.
2. No commercial use without permission.

Do you record in a professional studio

Not in a professional studio, but I use good equipment and always strive to improve audio quality.

Are there official social media accounts

Yes, you can find my verified accounts on the homepage. Beware of fake pages.

Is the channel affiliated with any religious organization

No, the channel is personal and operated by me, Obadah Mohammad Adnan, in accordance with Islamic guidelines.
